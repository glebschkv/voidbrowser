# VoidBrowser Privacy Policy

**VoidBrowser collects no data. Period.**

## What we don't do

- No telemetry
- No analytics
- No crash reports
- No accounts
- No cloud sync
- No data collection of any kind — not even "anonymous" usage stats

## Local storage

Bookmarks and settings are stored locally on your machine, encrypted with SQLCipher. The encryption key is stored in your operating system's credential manager (Windows Credential Manager). We never see it.

## Browsing data

Cookies, history, cache, and all other browsing data are destroyed when you close the browser. Every session starts clean.

## Network requests

VoidBrowser only makes outbound requests that you initiate:

- **Page navigation** — sites you choose to visit
- **Filter list updates** — EasyList and EasyPrivacy from their public CDNs

That's it. There are no background requests, no phone-home endpoints, no update checks against our servers.

## We have no servers

There is nothing to subpoena, hack, or breach. We cannot comply with data requests because we have no data. We cannot be compelled to add backdoors because there is no infrastructure to add them to.

## WebView2 SmartScreen

Microsoft's WebView2 runtime enables SmartScreen by default, which sends URLs to Microsoft's servers. VoidBrowser disables SmartScreen to prevent this.
